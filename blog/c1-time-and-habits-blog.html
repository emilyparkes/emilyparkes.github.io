Reflections

10 August 2017

What timebox and habits techniques did you try?

I have tried meditating in the morning and that is working well on getting me focused for the day. I also try meditating at night to wind down for the day and to help me get a restful sleep. I also use the Pomodoro Technique which has always worked well for me.

What did you find interesting?

I found the videos about creating habits really interesting as most of the time people do talk about the attitude to create a habit more than creating a habit and having the attitude come along with it. I think that is a great way to create habits. Also that some people need to be counterintuitive ways to be productive - I'm someone that works really well with creating lists and goals.

What worked for you?

The Pomodoro Technique. Meditiating in the morning and creating small habits.

Which will you experiement further with?

I am going to experiment more with meditating at night - I find it difficult to not pick up my phone when it's really late or to stop thinking. I also will experiement more with creating small habits.
